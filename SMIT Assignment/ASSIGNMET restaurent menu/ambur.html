Ambur Biryani

Ambur is a place in Tamil Nadu, India where this type of biryani is the most popular among all. Small grained rice known as “Seerage Samba Rice” is used to prepare it rather than the long basmati rice. The meat and spice are mixed with curd to give it a spicy taste. It is called “Pakki Biryani” because it is cooked in dum. This biryani is served with a spicy brinjal curry to boosts the taste of this delicious dish.
